## **Introduction**
For this project, I created a **DIY gift cart** using simple materials I already had at home. The idea was inspired by a few beautiful Pinterest pins, and I wanted to recreate something similar with my own twist.


<br>
</br>
## **Materials Used**
<br>
</br>
  - A box with a lid
  - Pink A4 size papers
  - Wooden skewers
  - Bottle caps
  - Black poster color / black glitter paper
  - Silver and rose gold glitter paper
  - Decorative bow
  - Glue


<br>
</br>
## **Step-by-Step Process**
<br>
</br>
### **1. Preparing the Base**

I used a box I already had and covered it neatly using a pink A4 sheet.

For wrapping the pink paper around the box, I followed this tutorial: https://youtu.be/QIb3R_O3Vrk?list=LL


<br>
</br>
### **2. Adding Support Structure**

To make the cart sturdy, I placed **wooden skewers** at all **four corners**
For better support, I **stuck two skewers together** before attaching them


<br>
</br>
### **3. Making the Wheels**

- I used **Bisleri bottle caps** as wheels
- Painted them using **black poster color** (acrylic paint works better)

*Pro Tip:* You can also use **black glitter paper** instead of paint to give the wheels a shinier finish!


<br>
</br>
### **4. Decorating the Cart**

This is the most fun part!

- I used **silver and purple glitter paper** for decoration
- Added a **small violet bow on top**, which I got from a cake box, it made the cart look even more adorable
