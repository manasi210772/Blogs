## **A Hot Evening at Thane Station**

It was a typical hot summer evening. The kind where the heat doesn’t fade even after the sun starts setting.

My friend and I were at Thane station, getting our blackbooks printed. The whole area was busy, crowded, and honestly… exhausting.


<br></br>
## **The Temptation**

While walking around, I noticed something everywhere — **sugarcane juice stalls**.
Almost every few steps, there was one. 
And at that point, I just couldn’t resist anymore.


<br></br>
## **The ₹20 Magic**

I stopped at a small stall located opposite the famous Alok hotel, right next to another juice center.

I ordered one large glass.

And then I noticed something interesting.

- First, the juice seller added a **layer of salt at the bottom of the glass**
- Then came **big cubes of ice**
- And finally, the freshly crushed **sugarcane juice**


<br></br>
## **The First Sip**

That first sip?
Instant relief.

It felt like my entire body cooled down in seconds. The sweetness of the sugarcane combined with that slight salty base created a surprisingly perfect balance.

It wasn’t just refreshing, it was energizing. 
And in that moment, nothing else mattered.


<br></br>
## **Simple, Affordable, Perfect**

The best part? It cost just **₹20**.

Something so simple, so accessible, yet so satisfying.

<br></br>
## **Final Thoughts**

Sometimes, it’s not fancy cafés or expensive meals that create memorable experiences.
Sometimes, it’s just:
- A hot evening  
- A tiring day  
- And a perfectly chilled glass of sugarcane juice  

And somehow… that’s enough
